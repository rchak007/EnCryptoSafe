Secure Your Seed Phrases with Confidence

Say goodbye to the stress of storing seed phrases directly. With our advanced encryption service, you can safely store your encrypted keys in the cloud, ensuring peace of mind and top-tier security. Split your seed phrase into 3 sets of words - Example- if you have 12 word seed phrase then split into 3 sets of 4 words each and encrypt each set separately. Then store each of your 3 sets - your AES key, AES Vector and Encrypted key of your seed phrase in different regular Cloud accounts where you can safely recover. Multi-factor your Cloud accounts. Best Practices for Securing Your Seed Phrase

Step 1
Secure AES Key & Vector

Store these in a primary cloud account (e.g., Google or Apple), and choose complex, password-like values.

Step 2
Divide & Encrypt

Encrypt smaller parts of your seed phrase, and store each encrypted segment in a separate cloud account.

Step 3
Enable Multi-Factor Authentication

Add MFA to all your cloud accounts to secure access and reduce unauthorized access risks.
